The Opportunity

Three reasons this
isn't just another job.

SaaS Labs sits at the intersection of AI, communication, and scale. You won't be a cog — you'll own things that matter.

01

You're building at the AI frontier

Our products — JustCall, AtomsAI, ServiceAgent, Helpwise, CallPage — are live, used by thousands of businesses daily, and increasingly AI-native. You'll work on real AI problems, not demo projects. We don't treat AI as a side experiment; we actively build it into how we think, operate, and scale.

02

You'll have real ownership from day one

We trust you to run with things. "Internalize CEO mindset" isn't a slogan — it's how decisions get made here. Every project, every risk, every outcome has someone's name on it. That name could be yours. We're lean enough that what you do visibly moves the needle.

03

You'll grow at the pace of the company

SaaS Labs is backed by Peak XV and growing fast. The company's trajectory means there are new challenges — and new roles — opening constantly. People who join early and perform don't stay in the same box for long. We promote from within, and we mean it.

Culture & Values

What makes us tick.

Our values aren't aspirational posters — they're the filter through which we hire, promote, and make decisions every day.

🤝

We believe in the power of working together. Brainstorming sparks breakthroughs, and every win belongs to the whole team — not just the person who shipped it.

📈

Raise the bar

Excellence isn't optional — it's the standard. We get better every day, push past "good enough," and never settle when "great" is within reach.

🧠

Internalize CEO mindset

We trust you to own your work and run with it. Every decision you make, every project you lead, every risk you take — it's yours to own. No hand-holding.

Perfect planning takes too long — we'd rather take imperfect action and iterate our way to excellence. Bias for doing, not discussing.

Customer-first isn't just a motto — it's how we work. We understand needs deeply, act with empathy, and obsess over making things better for everyone we serve.

🤖

If AI can, AI should

We actively build AI into how we think, operate, and scale. It's not a side experiment — it's the lens through which every team looks at their work.

Interview Process

Every great journey starts with a first step.

Here's what to expect after you apply. Most roles complete the full process in 3–4 weeks.

⏱️ ~3–4 weeks end to end
🎯 Focus on real skills
🤝 No trick questions
01
Application

Apply & Recruiter Screen

A short call to understand your background, what you're looking for, and whether this role is the right fit. A chance for both of us to ask questions.

02
Assessment

Skills-Based Task

A practical exercise relevant to the role — not an abstract puzzle, but something that resembles real work. A few days to complete at your own pace.

03
Interview

Hiring Manager Round

A deep dive into your experience, approach to problem-solving, and how you'd operate in the role. We want to understand how you think, not just what you've done.

04
Culture

Values & Leadership Round

A conversation with a senior leader focused on alignment with our values, working style, and long-term fit. This is also your chance to pressure-test us.

05
Offer

Reference Check & Offer

We'll speak with a couple of past colleagues, then evaluate your overall candidacy and make an offer. Welcome to the crew.

FAQs

Things candidates usually ask.

Still have questions? Your recruiter call is the best place to ask anything not covered here.

Is SaaS Labs remote-friendly?
+
We're hybrid by default. Most roles are based in our San Francisco or Noida offices, with flexibility built in — including up to 12 work-from-anywhere days per year. Specific remote arrangements depend on the role and team. Your recruiter will clarify during the screening call.
What does "AI-First" actually mean for day-to-day work?
+
It means AI is a first-class consideration in every decision — not just in product, but in how teams operate, how we write, how we analyse, and how we automate. You're expected to use AI tools actively and to look for ways to build AI into your workflows. If AI can do it, AI should.
How does compensation work?
+
We benchmark against top companies in our tier and aim to be competitive without playing games. Offers are based on role, level, location, and experience. We're transparent in the process — you'll know the range before you reach offer stage.
How quickly do people grow here?
+
Fast, if you perform. The company is growing quickly, which means new challenges and new roles open up constantly. We promote from within wherever we can. People who join early and take ownership tend to grow faster here than at most companies their size.
What is the culture around feedback?
+
Direct and candid. We don't do passive feedback — if something's off, you'll hear it quickly. Equally, if you're doing great work, you'll hear that too. We believe candour is a form of respect, and we try to practise it across all levels.
What does the assessment / take-home task involve?
+
It varies by role, but always resembles real work — not abstract puzzles. You'll typically be assessed on how you think through a problem, how you structure your approach, and the quality of your output. Full details are shared when you reach that stage. We respect your time and keep tasks scoped appropriately.
